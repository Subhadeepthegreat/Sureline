PUSHPAK Mk-I — The Original
Category: Launch Vehicle
Base Price: ₹12,00,00,000 (₹12 Crore)
Payload Capacity: 500 kg to LEO
Status: Active (Generation 1)

Named after the mythical flying chariot, Pushpak Mk-I was our first 
launch vehicle. Like most first attempts, it was beautiful, ambitious, 
and had a few "learning opportunities" (industry speak for "we had to 
chase it across 3 districts"). The Mk-I can deliver small satellites 
to Low Earth Orbit with a success rate we're now proud enough to 
actually disclose: 94%.

Best for: Budget-conscious clients who want their satellite in orbit 
without taking out a second mortgage.

PUSHPAK Mk-II — The Comeback Kid
Category: Launch Vehicle
Base Price: ₹18,50,00,000 (₹18.5 Crore)
Payload Capacity: 1,200 kg to LEO (300 kg to GTO)

The Mk-II is what happens when engineers are given a second chance

AKASH SHIELD — Space Debris Tracking
Category: Debris Tracking System
Base Price: ₹1,20,00,000 (₹1.2 Crore)

The system has prevented 12 collisions to date. Our insurance
