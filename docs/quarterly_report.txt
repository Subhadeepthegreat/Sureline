Q4 2025 (October – December 2025)

════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Q4 2025 was a blockbuster quarter for Mahakash. We launched 3 rockets, 
deployed 7 satellites, and the chai machine only broke once (a new 
record for uptime).

Key highlights:
• Total Revenue: ₹147.3 Crore (up 23% from Q3)
• Net Profit: ₹18.2 Crore (up from ₹12.5 Crore in Q3)
• EBITDA Margin: 19.4%
• Rockets Launched: 3 (all successful, we're on a streak)
• Samosas Consumed: 14,200 (also up 23%, suspiciously correlated)
• Employee Count: 82 (net +5 from Q3, -1 intern who "graduated 
  to a competitor" — we're handling it emotionally)
