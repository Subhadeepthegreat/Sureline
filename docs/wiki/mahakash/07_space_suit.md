The Gaganyaan Suit v2 is Mahakash's offering to the world of human spaceflight — designed in full collaboration with our Space Suit Design department, who are, against all reasonable expectation, the most fashionable team in Indian aerospace.

Base price: twenty-five lakh rupees per unit.

The suit handles thermal regulation across a range of minus one hundred and fifty degrees Celsius to plus one hundred and twenty degrees Celsius. It includes integrated biometric monitoring and is, by all accounts, surprisingly comfortable for a twelve-kilogram garment. It is available in three colours: Space White, Midnight Blue, and ISRO Orange.

It also has pockets. Because even astronauts need pockets. This was a non-negotiable design requirement.

The Space Suit Design team did submit a version to Lakme Fashion Week. They were politely declined. We do not bring this up anymore, but the fact that this page exists suggests it still weighs on us.
