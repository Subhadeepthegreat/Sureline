MAHASAT-1 is our flagship communication satellite and honestly our crown jewel. Category: Communication Satellite. Base price: four point five crore rupees per unit. Mass: three hundred and fifty kilograms. Design life: fifteen years — warranty voided if hit by space debris, which we say as a joke but also sort of mean.

Each MahaSat-1 comes with twenty-four Ku-band transponders, full Indian subcontinent coverage, and a handwritten "good luck" note from the Satellite Division team lead. Currently four MahaSat-1 units are in orbit. All four work. We are as surprised as you are.

One of them is streaming exactly eight hundred and forty-seven channels of cricket. This is not a bug. This is a feature.

MAHASAT-NANO is exactly what it sounds like — small. Category: Nano Satellite. Base price: eighty-five lakh rupees. Mass: twelve kilograms. Design life: five years.

The MahaSat-Nano is so small it was once mistaken for a lunchbox. One client literally received it via courier — in a Flipkart box. It still worked. We have since improved our packaging. The Nano is perfect for earth observation, IoT connectivity, and making your startup pitch deck say "we have a satellite in orbit."

Our first major Nano deployment was for AgriSat Corp in 2021 — farmers in their network got weather data forty percent faster, and crop yields went up fifteen percent. Samosa consumption at Mahakash HQ also went up fifteen percent that quarter. Correlation unclear.
